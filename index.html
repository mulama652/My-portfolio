About Me

The Skillful Journey

Based in Nairobi, I am Ian Mulama—a passionate, multi-skilled professional dedicated to excellence in education, design, and digital media. As a teacher trainee at Tangaza University, specializing in History and Kiswahili, I blend academic rigor with hands-on experience to deliver meaningful learning outcomes and creative solutions.

My teaching journey began with a transformative school practice at Nkaimurunya Comprehensive School (May–August 2024). Immersed in real classroom dynamics, I honed my ability to translate theory into practice—preparing schemes of work, delivering engaging lessons, and adapting to diverse learning needs. This experience strengthened my confidence, adaptability, and commitment to student success.

Continuing at the same school (January–April 2025), I deepened my expertise in classroom management, curriculum implementation, and effective communication. I embraced flexibility, patience, and creativity, ensuring every lesson was purposeful and impactful. These skills empower me to create positive, results-driven learning environments.

My commitment to service extends beyond the classroom. As a clerk with the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission (IEBC) during Kenya’s 2022 General Elections, I demonstrated integrity, precision, and resilience under pressure. This role sharpened my skills in public service, data management, and confidential operations—qualities I bring to every professional engagement.

Creativity is at the heart of my work. Serving on the media team at Karen Africa Gospel Church, I specialize in photography, video editing, and poster design. I capture moments, craft compelling visual stories, and deliver uplifting content that resonates. My technical proficiency in video production and graphic design ensures every message is communicated with clarity and artistry.

These diverse experiences have shaped me into a disciplined, empathetic, and innovative professional. I value teamwork, continuous learning, and using my skills to make a positive impact. Every project I undertake is guided by a commitment to quality, integrity, and client satisfaction.

As I advance in my career, I am dedicated to inspiring others, leading with purpose, and delivering solutions that exceed expectations. Let’s collaborate to bring your vision to life.
